Worst Nodes

Worst Nodes lists the nodes with low health scores in a subnet as measured by a node's status. Normal, warning, error, and critical status levels have the scores of 100, 80, 30, and 10 respectively. Click on a worst node to display more information about the node and view performance, topology, and other data.
