Multicast Forwarding

Multicast forwarding properties include information regarding the number of ports followed by the list of port numbers on a switch to which packets of a specific MLID value are forwarded. The MLID is the destination address for the packets, and packets sent to the MLID address will be sent to the ports listed for that MLID. This information is only available for switches and is not editable by the user.

*Other names and brands may be claimed as the property of others.
