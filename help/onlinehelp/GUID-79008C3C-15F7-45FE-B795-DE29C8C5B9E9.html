Events (Performance Subnet Summary)

The Event section displays past performance statistics based on recent events. The pie chart on the left summarizes the events distribution in a defined time window. This chart is very similar to the Status chart on Home tab, where nodes with informational or no events are categorized as normal nodes. Nodes without events are represented in gray. The bar chart on the right shows how the events distribute at each time point in the past.
