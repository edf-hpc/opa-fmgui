Port Properties

Port properties are not editable by the user.

To view the port properties:

  1. From the main screen, click the Performance tab.
  2. From the left resource panel, click the Device Types drop-down arrow to reveal the hierarchical view.
  3. For any node, click + (plus sign) to expand the tree and select a port for a host or a switch.
  4. Click the Properties tab.

*Other names and brands may be claimed as the property of others.
